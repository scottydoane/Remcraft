Welcome Atomic Ranch Readers!

This season's "in" color is "green!"

So why not add some energy conservation to your morning routine this holiday season? Remcraft's ECHO LED mirrors combine modern, energy-efficient LED illumination technology with classic styling that looks great in any environment, retro or not! ECHO LED shaving and make-up mirrors are available in vanity-top or wall-mounted models, featuring solid brass construction for lifelong durability and precision movements for smooth, easy adjustment with just the touch of a fingertip.

The bright 360º LED light ring bathes the user in uniform light, while offering reduced heat and energy consumption. What's more, the LED lighting system allows for a slim profile — the mirror body is just 1-1/4" deep — and its 50,000+ hour life means no more bulb changes!

ECHO LED mirrors also feature optical quality glass with 5x magnification and fog-free optics. Both models are available in luxurious chrome, bronze, or satin nickel finishes.
