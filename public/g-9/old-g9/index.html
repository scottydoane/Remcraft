G-9 Halogen Wall Series : Overview
 

 

Why Baci G-9 Halogen?

Baci’s G-9 Halogen Fixtures are designed around new technology, line-voltage G-9 halogen lamps. They offer all the advantages of traditional low-voltage halogen light sources — with none of the disadvantages (e.g., heat, noise, and electrical interference).

Baci's G-9 Halogen fixtures are as easy to install as any standard lighting fixture, can be dimmed with inexpensive conventional dimmers, and can be used with 25, 40, and 60 watt G-9 lamps (120 volt lamps for North America and 240 volt lamps for international applications). These compact fixtures are perfectly scaled for bathrooms, powder rooms, hallways, accent lighting, and more.

Don't miss Baci's new Murrina Glass G-9 Halogen Series, featuring hand blown, Venetian style, cased white opal glass shades with multi-colored millefiori accents. The die-cast canopy and heavy, turned fittings are solid brass and are available in chrome, brass, satin nickel or special finishes. These new fixtures are available in a wall-mounted sconce, and in two sizes of pendants with your choice of cord or stem suspension.

Also new to the Baci family are matching one-light sconce and three-light bathbar fixtures, as well as several exciting new table lamp offerings. For more detailed information, browse the G-9 Halogen Catalog.
