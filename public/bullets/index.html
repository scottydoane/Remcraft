RETRO by Remcraft Bullet Fixtures

For over half a century, Remcraft has been recognized as the leading manufacturer of indoor/outdoor bullet fixtures, and has earned an extraordinary reputation for innovative design, notable quality, and outstanding value. RETRO by Remcraft bullet fixtures are rapidly gaining "cult" status among fans of mid-century architecture, and are appearing in mid-century modern home renovation and restoration projects all over the country. Why buy a reproduction when you can have a brand-new original?

With handsome styling and durable, baked enamel finishes, Remcraft bullet fixtures are built to their original 1950's specifications and quality standards (with a few additional improvements) to ensure unmatched durability and long, trouble-free service. They are available in a wide variety of styles and finishes to suit any decor, indoors or out.
