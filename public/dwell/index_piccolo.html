Remcraft is pleased to announce new additions to our PICCOLO mirror series!

Rounding out the season this year are several new additions to our PICCOLO line of small make-up and shaving mirrors. PICCOLO mirrors include 3x magnified and plain (no magnification) glass, and come in six different styles — wall-mounted (permanent single or double swing-arm and temporary suction-cup) or multipurpose countertop vanity mirrors. Our new P-12 purse-sized compact with tortoise-shell inlay and our new smaller-size P-8 portable travel mirror make perfect holiday gifts. Most models are available in either gold or chrome finish.
