BACI L.E.D. Mirrors – Have the best of both worlds!

As a Dwell Magazine reader, you likely want products for your home that balance lasting beauty, solid construction and environmental consciousness. Now you can experience the familiar luxury of a Baci shaving and make-up mirror with a new, slim profile and the energy savings of LED illumination. Wall-mounted and vanity-top models offer crisp, clear light with a fraction of the usual heat and energy consumption. 50,000+ hour LEDs mean no bulbs to change! These new LED mirrors feature Baci’s solid brass construction and optical quality glass with 3x or 5x magnification — and are available in a number of designer finishes.
